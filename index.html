About me

I'm a senior attending Bullard Havens Technical High School enrolled in the Information Technology trade. During my time at my Bullard Havens I have obtained a variety of skills in the IT field. This year I was able to earn 5 certifications from Microsoft and I’m still going to get a couple more this year. I worked on a wide range of things IT involving both software and hardware. I’ve done programming in Python, JavaScript,Lua, and C(the programming language). I’ve fixed many computers and also worked with networking. Hands on. After High School, I plan on going to Housatonic Community college and earning a degree in Engineering Science then transfer and get a degree in either Computer Engineering or Electrical Engineering.
